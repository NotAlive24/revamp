**Business Continuity in Action**
It means keeping important work running during a disruption.

**Example**
A fire destroys the billing department overnight, but no one is hurt.

**Planning**
A BIA (Business Impact Analysis) had already identified billing as important and a backup work area was prepared.

**Response**
Customer service handled billing calls until the billing team resumed work.

**Result**
The company continued operating with little disruption, showing a successful BCP.
