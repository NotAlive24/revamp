Authentication is used to verify confidentiality of a user.
When a user logs in it is necessary to verify that the person is who they say they are.
There are three common methods of authentication : 

- Something you know: Passwords or passphrases.
- Something you have: Tokens, memory cards, smart cards.
- Something you are: Biometrics, measurable characteristics.

These can be used to verify the confidentiality of the user.
