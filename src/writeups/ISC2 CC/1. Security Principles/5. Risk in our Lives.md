## Personal Threats & Impacts

• Unauthorized Credit Card Charges: Can lead to frozen accounts, card replacement hassle, and updating bill payments.
• Mitigation: Avoid storing credit info on devices/browsers.
## Security Mitigation

• MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication): Adds security layers (username/password + email/phone code). Highly recommended by banks.
## Risk Transfer

• Travel Insurance: Covers prepaid expenses for cancellations (e.g., cruises due to weather).
• Other Insurance: Health coverage for accidents; Identity Theft policies.
• Provider Perspective: Companies manage financial risk by ensuring premiums exceed expected payouts.
