### Password Managers
Many systems require multiple passwords.  
**Password managers** store passwords so users do not have to remember all their security codes for different systems.
- The main disadvantage is the **risk of compromise** of the password manager.
### Security Risk
- Password managers may be protected by a **weak password or passphrase** (a longer password made of words).
- If compromised, **private data stored by cloud providers** may be accessed by unauthorized persons.
### Organizational Recommendation
- Organizations should encourage the use of **different passwords for different systems**.
- They should also provide a **recommended password management solution** for users.

---

## Poor Password Protection (To Avoid)

- **Reusing passwords** for multiple systems, especially using the same password for business and personal use.
- **Writing down passwords** and leaving them in unsecured areas.
- **Sharing passwords** with tech support or coworkers.
