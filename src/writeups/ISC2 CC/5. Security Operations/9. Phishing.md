## Phishing Attacks

Phishing attacks are used to target **individuals, entire departments, and companies**.  
This is a **significant threat** that security professionals need to be **aware of and prepared to defend against**.

- Many variations of basic phishing attacks have been developed in recent years.
- These attacks are deployed against individuals and networks through:
    - Emails
    - Phone calls
    - Spam
    - Instant messages
    - Videos
    - File attachments
    - Other delivery mechanisms
### Whaling Attacks
- Phishing attacks that try to trick **highly placed officials** or **private individuals with sizable assets** into authorizing large fund wire transfers to previously unknown entities are called **whaling attacks**.
